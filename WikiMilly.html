Milly

Milly, BLEHHHHH :P Cat or I Am Not Doing That Cat, is a viral photograph of a cat sticking its tongue out, often captioned with humorous dialogue in which it refuses to do things demanded of it. First posted by the Instagram account fatfatmillycat in July 2022, the photograph gained popularity in August 2022 following a viral tweet about homework, and contributed to the spread of the Silly Cats memes.
