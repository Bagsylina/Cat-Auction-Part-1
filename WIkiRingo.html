Ringo

Ringo takes the appearence of a plush cat, unable to move or speak, or do any sort of physical action. It can be easily mistaken for a toy cay. However, despite its' appearence, Ringo possesses extraordinary magical powers.

Among it's abilities are being able to read minds, psychic speaking, being able to nullify other magic attacks.

Ringo appears as sad most of the time, so it's good to sometimes cheer him on.
