The Four Horsemen of the Apocalypse

The Four Horsemen of the Apocalypse are the strongest Cats that have been spotted in the real world. They take the form of small kittens, making them seem innofensive. However they are very dangerous dispite their appearance.

They are thought to have been the cause of the apocalypse, though it's not a proven fact. They seem to have taken the side of humans, despite not having the best opinions of them. They might side with humans, but if they are bothered too much, they can become hostile.

The Four Horsemen are, in order, Pestilence, War, Famine and Little Richard. Most of the times they act alone, but in very rare occasions they can be seen meeting eachother. Their true intentions are unknown, so it's best to be wary of them.

Origin

The most likely origin of the Four Horsemen is from hell itself, due to their extremely strong powers compared to all other Cats and their uniqueness.

Characteristics of each Horsemen

Pestilence

This kitten represents the embodiment of disease and plague. It's fur is orange with white spots.

It's powers are to inflict great sickness against its' opponent, with no current cure.

War

This kitten represents the embodiment of war and battles. It has a very violent attitude, being the most dangerous out of the four. It's fur is white and grey.

It's powers consist of deadly attacks and making weapons out of any material. People around it seem more prone to fighting and violent outbursts.

Famine

This kitten represents the embodiment of hunger. It seems to always seek food, being capable of eating almost anything. It is a tabby kitten with white spots.

It's powers are to inflict great hunger on its' opponent, almost on the verge of death.

Little Richard

This kitten represents the embodiment of all Richards. It seems very polite and calm compared to the other Horsemen. It's fur is black, gray and kitten.

Not much is know about it, except for the fact that it's quite friendly towards humans.
