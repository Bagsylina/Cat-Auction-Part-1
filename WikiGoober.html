Goober

Goober is an extremely active Cat, that enjoys running and jumping a bit too much, causing it to be too agile.

Beacause of it's high agility, it may be a bit dangerous to it's user, accidentaly harming you.

This type of Cat is also very unpredictable due to its' chaotic movements, sometimes being uncontrollable.

Its' energetic behaviour can also be a plus for owners that look for a playful and protective Cat.

Origin

It's origin is unknown, having been seen in multiple locations all around the globe.

It can also appear as any breed and age, unlike many other types of Cats.

Special skills

⚠️

Goober runs around chaotically, hitting random enemies, and sometimes, accidentaly, teammates.
