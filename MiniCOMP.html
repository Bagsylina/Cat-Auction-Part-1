The Mini COMP

The basic needs for a person

Features

Compact Form

It's small and lightweight design makes it easy to carry around in a pocket, especially since you don't have to put it on your arm.

No connectivity

Unlike the Arm COMP, the Mini COMP doesn't need to stay connected to your own body.

Summoning on the go

The summoning is made easier than it ever was, now with no Magnetite needed.

Cat Auction

You can now access the Cat Auction directly from your COMP, while still being able to buy and sell Cats, without needing to plug it into a computer.

Contact other people

You can call, message and accept requests from other people.

See your location on the Map

You can access the COMP Map to check nearby locations and danger reports from other people, as well as plan your routes.

Missing Features

  • You cannot use Terminals unless you buy a Terminal Connector.
  • No magic enhancedments, your only defence are your summoned Cats.
  • No Area Scan. You'll only depend on the basic COMP Map and danger reports.
