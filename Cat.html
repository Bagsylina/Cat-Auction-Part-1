What are Cats?

Cats are your buddies in this messed-up world. They appeared at the same time the apocalypse started, serving as our own protectors.

Cats appear as a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. Don't worry they don't eat humans. They are dommesticated, but the relationship with it's COMP user differences greatly depending on its' type and personality.

They are valued for their magical abilities and apparent friendlyness to humans, being able to fight against Demons.

How do they work?

You are able to regulary spot them in the world and you can attract them using food, treats and toys, but usually its' decided if it joins you by the Cat Distribution System.

Once the cat has considered you as it's owner, you are able to store it in your COMP or just take it with you physically on your adventures.

You can use your COMP to summon it at any time you want.

What is the Cat Auction

The Cat Auction is another place where from where you can get Cats, available on normal PCs and Laptops and now on the most recent generation of COMPs.

You can sell or buy Cats on there, or bet on them like in a normal auction, with the highest better getting the Cat.

While it may look unethical, it does help new COMP users to obtain them without risking their lives on the field.

The Cat Wiki

The Cat Wiki is a website where you can learn about all the different kinds of Cats.

It provides description of the Cats, along with their stats and behavior, being useful in recruiting them. It also describes their unique skills and abilities, useful in creating the perfect party for your needs.
